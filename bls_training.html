Basic Life Support (BLS) Training

The BLS Training project by Rotaract Club of Sukedhara focuses on teaching critical first aid and life-saving skills to community members. The training covers techniques such as CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation), how to handle choking, and the use of AED (Automated External Defibrillators).

Objectives:

  • Provide hands-on training to over 50 participants
  • Spread awareness about first aid and emergency response
  • Collaborate with local hospitals for certifications
