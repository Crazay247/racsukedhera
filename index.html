About Rotaract Club of Sukedhara

Rotaract Club of Sukedhara (RAC Sukedhara) is a dynamic youth-led club under Rotary International, dedicated to fostering community service, professional development, and leadership skills among youth. Founded on the principles of "Service Above Self," RAC Sukedhara has been an active contributor to various community service projects and initiatives that make a tangible difference in society.

Our Mission

To empower young people to take leadership roles in serving their communities while fostering fellowship and collaboration across diverse sectors.

Our Vision

To create a vibrant and inclusive community where youth are empowered to lead through innovation, creativity, and compassion.

Our Projects

Jeewan Twatha: Donation to the Monks of Shreegha Baha

Collaborated with the Rotaract Club of Sukedhara and supported singer Minarwa Devi Shakya Bajracharya to promote a culturally significant Newari music video. Donated food, clothing, and blankets to the monks of Shreegha Baha.

Youth in Climate Justice

An awareness campaign on climate change featuring Sanjay Panthi, discussing the 3Rs (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) and the impact of global lobbying on climate change regulations. Held at New Summit College.

Zonal Teej Celebration

A cultural event hosted at Liberty College with seven other clubs, celebrating Teej with performances, music, and fellowship. Treasurer Sushovan Shakya performed a live song.

Gift of Hope 3.0

On National Constitution Day, RAC Sukedhara donated kitchen materials to Anmol Bal Bikash Griha. The project enhanced kitchen sanitation and aimed to fulfill the needs of the children at the orphanage.

Basic Life Support (BLS) Training

Organized BLS training sessions to equip community members with essential life-saving skills, ensuring a safer community.

Global Harmony: Conflict Resolution for World Peace

In coordination with Rtr. Sejeena Pradhan, this project aimed to facilitate discussions on global peace and conflict resolution, with a focus on fostering harmony worldwide.
